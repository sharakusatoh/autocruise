# Task Recipe: Browser Market Research

Use a browser to collect decision-useful facts from web sources without drifting into unnecessary browsing.

## Best Used When

- A user wants quick competitor or market research
- Product pricing or feature comparison is needed
- Public information must be summarized from several pages

## Inputs

- Research topic
- Required facts
- Preferred sources or exclusions
- Output destination

## Success Conditions

- Relevant sources were opened
- Requested facts were extracted
- Information is copied or summarized in the requested place

## Prompt Examples

- `Chromeで競合3社の料金ページを見て、主な違いをメモ帳に整理して。`
- `Edgeで製品比較を調べて、営業向けに3つの要点をWordへ下書きして。`

## Tips

- Tell it exactly what facts matter: price, plan, feature, deadline, contact form, and so on
- Limit the number of sources if speed matters
- If no external submission should happen, state “research only”
