# Task Recipe: Word Meeting Minutes Cleanup

Turn rough notes into readable meeting minutes in Word with headings, bullets, and clear action items.

## Best Used When

- A salesperson needs presentable minutes after a customer call
- Internal meeting notes are messy and need structure
- Action items must be separated from discussion notes

## Inputs

- Document path or new document instruction
- Meeting title and date
- Raw notes or the section to rewrite
- Save or export instruction

## Success Conditions

- The minutes have a clear title and section structure
- Decisions and action items are easy to identify
- Names, dates, and owners remain accurate

## Prompt Examples

- `Wordで会議メモを整形して、決定事項と次回アクションを分けてまとめて。`
- `議事録ドラフトを読みやすく直して、箇条書き中心で整理して。送付前で止めて。`

## Tips

- Mention whether the style should be formal, internal, or customer-facing
- Ask for owner and due-date formatting if action items matter
- If the raw notes are long, specify the exact section to clean first
