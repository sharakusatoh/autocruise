# PowerPoint App Profile

Build and revise slide decks while keeping slide order, formatting, and speaker intent intact.

## Purpose

PowerPoint is best for client decks, weekly updates, internal briefings, and proposal presentations.

## Main Screens

- Slide canvas
- Thumbnail pane
- Ribbon
- Notes pane
- Presenter or export dialogs

## Typical Tasks

- Update text on existing slides
- Replace screenshots or charts
- Reorder slides
- Export deck to PDF

## Important Shortcuts

- `Ctrl+M`
- `Ctrl+D`
- `Ctrl+Shift+C`
- `Ctrl+Shift+V`
- `F5`
- `Shift+F5`

## Common Pitfalls

- Editing the wrong slide due to thumbnail focus
- Accidentally changing a master or layout
- Distorting images by dragging without aspect awareness
- Exporting the wrong version

## Operational Notes

- Confirm slide number and title before editing
- Prefer small edits on existing layouts instead of rebuilding by hand
- Re-observe after each text or image replacement to catch overflow
- Delete slides and overwrite saves when the request identifies the intended deck and output

## Prompt Tips

- Specify slide number or slide title whenever possible
- Mention whether to keep the existing style or only update content
- For sales decks, include audience, objective, and whether the deck should be presentation-ready or draft-only
