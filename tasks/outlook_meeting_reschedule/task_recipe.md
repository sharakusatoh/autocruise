# Task Recipe: Outlook Meeting Reschedule

Move or update an Outlook meeting while preserving attendees, subject, and calendar clarity.

## Best Used When

- A meeting must be moved to a new time
- A calendar hold needs to be extended or shortened
- A sales call requires a revised invite with updated notes

## Inputs

- Meeting title
- Original or approximate date and time
- New schedule
- Whether to send the update immediately

## Success Conditions

- The correct meeting entry was opened
- Date, time, or notes were updated correctly
- Update delivery matches the instruction

## Prompt Examples

- `Outlookで明日のA社定例会を30分後ろ倒しにして、更新送信して。`
- `営業会議の予定を来週火曜15時に変更して、本文に会議室変更も追記して。`

## Tips

- Mention attendee or customer names if similar meetings exist
- If the meeting should not be sent yet, say "save only"
- When rescheduling across days, include the full target date to avoid ambiguity
