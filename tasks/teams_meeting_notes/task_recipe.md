# Task Recipe: Teams Meeting Notes

Collect or draft meeting notes from Teams without accidentally posting unfinished content.

## Best Used When

- Notes from a Teams meeting need to be captured
- A summary should be drafted in chat or copied elsewhere
- Key decisions and action items must be extracted

## Inputs

- Meeting or chat identity
- Output destination
- Required sections such as decisions, blockers, next steps
- Draft-only or post instruction

## Success Conditions

- The correct meeting or chat is open
- Notes capture the requested points
- The result is drafted, copied, or posted as instructed

## Prompt Examples

- `Teamsの会議メモを開いて、決定事項と次アクションを整理して下書きだけ作って。`
- `Teamsチャネルの会議要約をまとめて投稿して。投稿前に内容を見直して。`

## Tips

- Say whether the output should stay in Teams or be copied to another app
- If posting is sensitive, ask it to stop at draft
- Mention the preferred note structure explicitly
